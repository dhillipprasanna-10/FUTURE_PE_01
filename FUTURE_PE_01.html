Our Promise to You

Founded by passionate coffee lovers, Brew Haven was created to bring people together through exceptional coffee, warm hospitality, and meaningful experiences. Every visit is designed to feel comfortable, familiar, and inspiring.

We source every bean with intention — from the misty slopes of Coorg, the valleys of Araku, the estates of Chikmagalur — and put them in the hands of SCA-trained baristas who treat extraction like an art form.

Whether you're here for your third double-shot this week or stepping in for the very first time, you'll find the same thing: a space that feels like it was made for you.

Our Story

More Than
a Coffee Shop

Brew Haven began with a simple dream: to create a café where exceptional coffee and meaningful conversations could exist under one roof. What started as a passion for sourcing India's finest beans has grown into a welcoming destination for students, professionals, families, and coffee enthusiasts alike. Every cup served here reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and craftsmanship. We partner with trusted estates across Coorg, Araku, and Chikmagalur, ensuring every bean is ethically sourced and expertly roasted. Beyond coffee, Brew Haven is a community space where ideas are shared, friendships are formed, and memorable moments are created every day.
